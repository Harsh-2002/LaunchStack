Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our n8n hosting service.

What is n8n?

n8n is a workflow automation tool that allows you to connect different services and automate tasks. It's like Zapier or Integromat, but with more flexibility and can be self-hosted.

Why should I use your hosting instead of self-hosting?

Our hosting service saves you time and resources on server management, updates, and maintenance. We provide reliable hosting with up to 95% uptime on Pro plans, automatic updates, backups, and dedicated support so you can focus on building your workflows.

How does the waitlist work?

After joining our waitlist, we'll review your application and reach out to you with next steps. We're currently in a controlled rollout phase to ensure the highest quality service for all our customers.

Can I migrate my existing n8n workflows?

Yes! We provide a seamless migration process for your existing n8n workflows. Our team will guide you through the entire process to ensure a smooth transition.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include some level of support. Starter plans come with community support, Pro plans include priority email support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

We currently don't offer a free trial, but we do offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service.
